The Trees of the Concrete Jungle

An interactive guide to the trees of the City of Melbourne

About This Map

This interactive map displays the trees of Melbourne's CBD, visualised by their maturity levels and their size (taken from diameter at breast height). The data for individual tree location and information comes from the City of Melbourne's open data portal. For the parkland areas, the data was sourced from Vic.Data meshblock categories. The tree guard data is from the street furniture dataset by data.vic

How To Use This Map

  • Use the search bar to find trees in specific locations
  • Click on individual trees for detailed information
  • Use the legend filters to show/hide different tree types
  • Zoom in/out to explore different areas
